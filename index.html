$HPM Token

$HPM is an BEP20 token which started on the Binance Smart Chain in March 10, 2022. The max supply is 10 billion and one of its key functionalities is its decentralized usage rewards mechanism. For every active user transaction a 5% $HPM reward is granted to holders’ decentralized wallets only, fostering a healthy distributed network and community usage. The more $HPM is actively used, the more rewards are granted!.

Buyback Wallet

3% tokens are collected from every transaction and converted to BNB and are stored in Our contract. The wallet is known as Buyback Wallet. Which when enabled purchases $HPM directly from exchanges and removes the purchased tokens permanently from circulating supply while simultaneously making green candles on the price chart.

Liquidity Pool

2% of every transaction is converted into liquidity for Pancake Swap. Pancake swap fixes the low liquidity problems of DEXs through the liquidity Pool mechanism. Our Smart contract deposits 2% from every buy/sell transaction as $HPM and $BNB into a liquidity pool. At the same time, 1% from every transaction is allocated to Marketing to fuel the most ambitious projects in our Ecosystem and reward the active community.
